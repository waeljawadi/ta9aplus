   The overall objective of We love Kairouan :

Develop the image of Kairouan cultural, environmental, tourism and socialization of young people in associative work and the development of a sense of volunteerism.

    We have committed ourselves to the following :

- Ensure the independence of the Assembly from the political and partisan employment.
- Do not take the responsibility of leadership involved in a political party.
- The adoption of the principle of transparency and clarity in the funding and programming processes.

   Association aims to:

- Contribute to the scene of urban sophistication and activate the cultural life of the city of Kairouan.
- Contribute to beautify the face of Kairouan and assist in their environment and green areas care..
- To contribute to the legacy of physical and moral care of the city in an effort to stimulate cultural tourism..
- Contribute to enriching the associative fabric through the involvement of associations in all activities of the Association.
- Contribute to the strengthening of the international network of associations: We love ...

Association consists of:

- Members of the governing body: 7 members
- Active members
- About 30 volunteer members

Means and mechanisms:

- Demonstrations and field visits
- Training programs and training courses.
- And scientific lectures and seminars.
- Open for reflection and dialogue rings.
- The preparation of studies and projects.

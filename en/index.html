Local Youth Forum
About alternative energy policy in Kairouan


    Project Objectives :

  The project seeks to stimulate the local authority to pay attention to the energy sector and aims to socialization of young people by organizing a forum provides an opportunity for dialogue and debate between young people and representatives of local authorities and experts in the field of energy and to support the capacity of young people participating in the field of alternative energy policies.

 The project also aims to raise awareness of the components of civil society and local media the importance of alternative energy policies by organizing a press conference and campaign advocacy related to the subject.

   Project activities:

- Local Youth Forum.
- A photographic exhibition of Kairouan.
- Photos gallery Council of People's Congress .
- A training course in advocacy .
- Advocacy campaign .
- Press conference.
